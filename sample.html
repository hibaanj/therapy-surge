About Us

A group of Qualified Profesional graduates bring to you Islamic Therapy and counselling for sisters only, one on one counselling offering help world wide. , Our aim is to provide Shariah-complaint, psychological counselling and therapy to our sisters in need — to help you protect your heart, strengthen your eemaan and enable you to envision and evaluate choices in a way that aid you in building stability and security, in both Deen and Dunya. Our clients develop life-changing habits to help them cope with any of life’s various obstacles. Our therapy sessions are designed to understand personal issues and provide clients with effective tools to create a positive change in their live. We are making therapy affordable. The sessions are on call and chat based.
